Strands

STEM

The STEM strand is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. It prepares students for careers in scientific research, engineering, computer science, and other related fields by offering advanced courses in these subjects.

ABM

The ABM strand focuses on equipping students with the knowledge and skills necessary for careers in accountancy, business, and management. Students learn about financial management, business operations, marketing, and entrepreneurship, preparing them for roles in the corporate world or as entrepreneurs.

HUMSS

The HUMSS strand is tailored for students interested in the fields of humanities and social sciences. It includes subjects such as history, literature, political science, sociology, and psychology. This strand prepares students for careers in education, law, social work, and other related fields.

GAS

The GAS strand is a flexible option for students who are still exploring their interests and career paths. It offers a broad range of subjects from various strands, providing a well-rounded education that prepares students for a variety of college courses and career opportunities.

ICT

The ICT strand focuses on the study of information technology and communications. It covers topics such as computer programming, web development, network administration, and digital media. This strand prepares students for careers in IT, software development, and other tech-related fields.

HE

The HE strand is designed for students interested in home management, culinary arts, hospitality, and other related fields. It includes courses on food preparation, nutrition, housekeeping, and family resource management, preparing students for careers in the hospitality industry and home economics education.

SMAW

The SMAW strand focuses on skills and knowledge related to industrial arts, including welding, carpentry, electronics, and automotive technology. Students receive hands-on training in these areas, preparing them for technical and vocational careers in the industrial sector.

DRESS MAKING

The Dress Making strand emphasizes skills in designing, creating, and altering clothing and textiles. It includes courses on fashion design, sewing techniques, fabric selection, and garment construction. Students are prepared for careers in fashion design, tailoring, and the textile industry.
